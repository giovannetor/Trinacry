POKER HELP!!
================================================
Commands:

1)  .ttt = starts the match. If started, joins you to the match.
2)  .join  = join you to the match.
3)  .grid = shows the grid.
4)  .quit = leave the match
5)  .play = plays in a box. Syntax is better explained below.

================================================
### Syntax Hints ###

The grid works this way:

        A1 | A2 | A3
       ___________
        B1 | B2 | B3
       ___________
        C1 | C2 | C3

Using .play B3 will result in this:

        A1 | A2 | A3
       ___________
        B1 | B2 |  X
       ___________
        C1 | C2 | C3
